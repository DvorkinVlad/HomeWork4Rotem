# Heroes of Might and Magic III

A modern fan page dedicated to **Heroes of Might and Magic III**, the beloved 1999 turn-based strategy game that revolutionized the genre.

## About This Project

This is an informational website exploring the core concepts, gameplay mechanics, and enduring legacy of one of gaming's most iconic strategy titles. The site provides an in-depth look at what made Heroes III a genre favorite and why it continues to captivate players nearly 25 years after its release.

## Features

- **Overview** - Introduction to the game and its significance in gaming history
- **Gameplay Guide** - Detailed breakdown of core mechanics including:
  - Hero progression system
  - Town management and development
  - Tactical battlefield strategies
  - Resource collection and management
- **Legacy Section** - Exploration of the game's lasting impact on the strategy genre

The project features a dark fantasy-themed interface inspired by the game's aesthetic, with:
- Gradient backgrounds evoking the game's atmospheric environments

## Getting Started

1. Clone this repository
2. Open `index.html` in your web browser

No build process or dependencies required - just open and play!

## About Heroes of Might and Magic III

**Release Date:** May 1999  
**Developer:** New World Computing  
**Genre:** Turn-based Strategy  
**Platform:** Originally PC, now available on modern platforms

Heroes III is considered a masterpiece of strategy gaming, featuring deep gameplay mechanics, memorable campaigns, and a passionate community that continues to create mods and content.
