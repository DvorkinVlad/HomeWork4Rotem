About the Game

Heroes of Might and Magic III is a classic turn-based strategy game published by 3DO and developed by New World Computing. It was released in 1999 and quickly became one of the most beloved fantasy strategy titles.

The game blends hero development, army management, town building, and exploration in a fantasy world filled with castles, monsters, and powerful artifacts.

Why It Matters

Heroes 3 is best known for its deep tactical combat, memorable factions, and rich campaign stories. It is often praised for replay value and strong community support even decades later.
