How Heroes 3 plays

Discover the core mechanics that make Heroes of Might and Magic III a strategic favorite, from hero progression and town management to tactical battlefields and resource control.

Core Gameplay

Heroes 3 is a turn-based game where players control heroes, recruit armies, and conquer towns. Each turn, players move heroes across a map filled with treasure, enemies, and resources.

Key Elements

  • Heroes: Characters who lead armies and gain experience.
  • Towns: Bases where players build structures and hire creatures and heroes.
  • Resources: Gold, wood, ore, sulfur, crystal, gems, and mercury are used to build and recruit.
  • Combat: Battles take place on a tactical grid with six unit stacks.

Game Modes

Players can choose from single-player campaigns, standalone scenarios, or hotseat/multiplayer matches. The game is praised for its balanced factions and varied strategy options.
